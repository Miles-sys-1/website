First place in the intellectual game "Zakovat"

On April 14, 2025, the intellectual game "Zakovat" was held, organized by UEnter and Teach for Uzbekistan. In this prestigious competition, students from leading international higher education institutions in Tashkent competed in knowledge and thinking: TOBB ETU Tashkent University, Westminster International University in Tashkent, Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent, Webster University in Tashkent, and Management Development Institute of Singapore in Tashkent (MDIS Tashkent). The students took the honorary first place.
